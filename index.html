about me

Hi! I'm Snipta! I'm a UT Dallas undergrad double majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Science.

I love developing creative solutions to complex problems by drawing on my interdisciplinary background and experiences in technology, neuroscience, healthcare, and public policy!

I am particularly interested in the intersection of healthcare and technology, because innovations in this area are really exciting and have the potential to change millions of lives by increasing access to equitable care.
